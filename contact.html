Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open an account?

You can open an account online in just a few minutes. Click on "Sign Up" and follow the instructions to get started.

What documents do I need?

You'll need a government-issued ID and proof of address. We'll guide you through the process during signup.

How secure is my money?

We use bank-level security with 256-bit encryption and FDIC insurance up to $250,000 per account.

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, and we'll send instructions to your email address.

Are there any hidden fees?

We believe in transparent pricing. There are no hidden fees, and we'll always notify you before any charges.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us by phone, email, or through the contact form on this page. We're here to help 24/7.
